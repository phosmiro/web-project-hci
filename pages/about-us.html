Who Are We

BIT5 is a student-led development team dedicated to simplifying the dormitory search process in Dagupan. Our website offers a streamlined platform for students to find comfortable and affordable accommodations, crafted by students who understand the challenges firsthand. Join us in our mission to empower students with the tools they need for a successful academic journey.
